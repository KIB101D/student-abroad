Kyiv National Economic University
named after Vadym Hetman

LOCATION: Kyiv, Ukraine  
CONTACTS: ciam@kneu.edu.ua  

Kyiv National Economic University named after Vadym Hetman is focused on participation in international researches and education, and therefore endeavours to maintain its relations with other countries. At the University international activity is coordinated by the Center of International Academic mobility (CIAM). Established in 1998 as a Centre of International Relations, the Center was renamed as the Centre of International Academic Mobility in 2015. CIAM is a structural subdivision of the University, which carries out the planning, organization and monitoring of the international academic relations between the KNEU and foreign higher education establishments, research institutions, organizations, foundations, institutions, grant makers and businesses.
